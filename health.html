Health Centers

Qatif Central Hospital

The hospital contains more than 500 beds, and offers high levels of medical services to all patients, taking into account the need to maintain the privacy and feelings of patients as well as the respect and commitment to them. The hospital also aims to provide educational and medical training for physicians working in different fields.

The hospital is located on the Dhahran-Jubail Highway, and because of its location, it has played a major role in receiving and treating most cases of road accidents and diversion from various cities and villages in the Eastern Region.

Al-Zahra General Hospital

The hospital has pioneered the concept of specialized clinics in the Kingdom. All clinics are provided with the updated and required devices to provide appropriate patient management. These clinics are staffed by a team of special consultants who work 8 hours a day and with on-call service in case of emergency.

The department of emergency and ambulance aims to provide an emergency care, ambulance transportation, and respond to disasters through specialized unique team, who works 24/7, through the control unit with emergency services and the use of facilities for rapid response and rescue life and reduce the injury by availability of modern fleet of highly equipped.

Mouwasat Hospital - Qatif Branch

A branch of the Mouwasat hospitals chain is located in Qatif in the heart of Al-Mashari. This branch is equipped with technical building designs, modern facilities and modern infrastructure. The hospital also provides doctors with residential quarters and has a car park facility for visitors.

Its capacity is 100 beds divided into single and double rooms as well as deluxe suites. The total number of outpatient clinics is 40 distributed across the various medical specialties.
